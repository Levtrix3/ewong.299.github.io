Search and Destroy

Now if Vivienne Westwood is too expensive for ya, heres a cool place with alternative prices (Some things are a bit hefty in price though but there are some real gems in here that are hard to find) This place gives off a very vintage underground punk vibe that could just be a cool place to visit. The decor ranges from baby heads, sex dolls, hanging body bags, a plethora of dildos and nasty-body-parts attatched to random things. If this place is too extreme for ya you can head around the same block that this store is located on and explore the vendors and tattoo shops!
